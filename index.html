PHILOSOPHY

I seamlessly blend hip-hop lyrics into everyday conversation, and cite my sources. I feed off of high energy collaboration and prefer face-to-face conversation over phone calls. Thirsty for knowledge, I collect (and read) old books about history, philosophy, & archeology - throwing away each and every dust jacket, with the firm belief that you should not judge a book by its cover. I am immensely passionate about creating innovative digital experiences and embrace the realization that my best work often happens after the end of a normal work day. I love what I do for a living.
